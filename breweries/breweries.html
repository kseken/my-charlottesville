Reason Beer

Website

Hours:

Tue-Thu 3-8ish

Fri-Sat 1-8ish

Sun 1-5ish

Flights:

4 "tasters" (6-7oz pours) will run you $12 (each taster is $3)

Food:

Chips & Salsa, Prosciutto, pub snacks

What you need to know:

  • Minimalist place with minimalist but great beer.
  • Typically about 5-7 beers on tap without much pomp and cricumastance. But, while there may be fewer choices, they are all done very well.
  • A little hidden up on Route 29 near Stonefield. Pairs well with a trip to Al Carbon Chicken (~5 min drive away).
  • Devon, the person most likely serving you your beer, is a joy and will infect you with his love of the beer in 0.5 seconds.
  • Bonus non-alcoholic taps: Snowing In Space cold brew coffee, plus a local kombucha.
  • In-depth article/interview

What you need to know:

  • Popular for "$2 Tuesdays" (get a 12oz pour of any beer under 8% ABV for $2).
  • Quite a large space. Works well for big groups!
  • Finding free parking may be tricky since it is (basically) downtown, but it is next to ample Per Hour paid parking.
  • No outside seating.
  • Annual membership option exists; inquire at the bar for details.
  • Build-your-own Bloody Mary bar on Sundays.
  • Large bar seating area as well as lounge-y seating near fireplace-type-thing.
